How it works...

  • Online bidding closes at 12pm the day before the fair
  • Bids are anonymous, nobody will see your name or contact details on the website
  • There is a no minimum bid, you can bid what you like
  • If there is something you really want and you are unable to attend the fair, place your maximum bid to hopefully secure it
  • Bidding will continue on the day at the fair
  • Once the bidding is closed at 3.15pm on the day of the fair, winners who attend the fair will be expected to pay and collect their winning items at 3.45pm
  • If there is lots of frenzied competitive bidding at the close of an item, we will go to sealed bids
  • After the raffle draw at 3.20pm in the Main Hall - three lots from the auction will go into a live auction
  • If you are unable to attend the fair but win via your online bid someone will be in touch to arrange collection and payment
  • Where there is more than one item available for a lot, all top bidders will win the item
  • All items must be paid for in person by 4pm (Cash, Card or Cheque) unless you are unable to attend or leave early
  • Please pay attention to any restrictions in the descriptions
  • It is not possible to exchange or redeem items for money
  • For more information about a Lot click on its image or title
